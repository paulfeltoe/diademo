About Your Sinuses

  • Air-filled cavities in facial bones
  • Natural mucus production daily
  • Help filter and humidify air
  • Connected to nasal passages
  • Located in cheeks, forehead, between eyes

Common Contributing Factors

  • Seasonal changes
  • Environmental irritants
  • Changes in air pressure
  • Dry or very humid air
  • Smoke exposure
  • Swimming or diving

Preventive Measures

  • Regular hand washing
  • Using air purifiers
  • Maintaining good humidity levels (30-50%)
  • Avoiding known irritants
  • Regular exercise
  • Adequate sleep
  • Proper hydration

Helpful Self-Care Tips

  • Warm compresses on face
  • Gentle facial massage
  • Staying upright when sleeping
  • Using a humidifier at night
  • Nasal saline rinses
  • Steam inhalation
  • Regular physical activity
